The PotLucky Journey

A potluck app that allows you to coordinate who’s bringing what to an event - potluck, craft fair, party, convention, tailgate, whatever!
We’ve all been to that party with five bags of chips and no dessert. More dip than you know what to do with, but only one main dish. Way too much food, but not enough plates or silverware. Gotten to the convention or craft fair and *someone* forgot the folding table. Never wonder who’s bringing the kielbasa and who’s bringing the karaoke machine again with “PotLucky”!
